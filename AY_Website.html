What is a bird?

A bird is a warm-blooded vertebrate with feathers and wings. They lay hard-shelled eggs and have a toothless beak.

Where do birds live?

Birds live all over the globe in every continent. While some nest in trees, others are perfectly happy on the ground, snow, or water.

What do birds eat?

Different types of bird eat different things. This includes:

  • Seeds
  • Fish
  • Krill
  • Worms
  • Bugs
  • Mice
